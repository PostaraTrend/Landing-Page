IRAP Application — Technical Resources

Architectural walkthrough of the four R&D decision loci

Welcome. This page hosts technical orientation material that accompanies the formal Statement of R&D Uncertainty submitted with our IRAP application. It is intended for the assigned Industry Technology Advisor and any technical reviewer who would benefit from a guided walkthrough of the autonomous editorial decision-making layer of the PostaraTrend platform.

i
Best experienced on desktop with audio enabled. The walkthroughs use browser-based speech synthesis (Web Speech API). For best results, please use Chrome, Edge, or Safari. Read-along transcripts are linked below for anyone unable to use audio.

Walkthroughs

Recommended for pre-call ≈ 10 minutes

D1–D4 Walkthrough — The Four R&D Uncertainties

A focused, ten-minute walkthrough of the four decision loci that anchor the R&D scope of the proposed project. For each locus, the walkthrough states the technical uncertainty, explains why it cannot be resolved through engineering judgment alone, and identifies the project phase that addresses it. This is the recommended pre-call orientation asset.
